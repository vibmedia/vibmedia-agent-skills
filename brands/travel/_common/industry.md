# Travel Industry Context

## Market Overview
- India's domestic travel market: ₹15 lakh crore (2025)
- Youth adventure segment growing 25% YoY
- WhatsApp dominant communication channel (500M+ users in India)
- Group travel (3-8 people) is the fastest growing segment among 18-35 age group

## Key Competitors
- **Thrillophilia** — Market leader, ₹500Cr+ revenue, broad inventory
- **WanderOn** — Strong Instagram presence, youth-focused branding
- **JustWravel** — Budget trips, student community, volunteer model
- **Trek The Himalayas** — Expert trekking, safety-first reputation
- **Zostel** — Hostel + trip combos, backpacker community

## Customer Behavior
- Research on Instagram/YouTube → DM on WhatsApp → Book via UPI
- Group trips (3-8 people) most popular
- Price sensitivity: ₹5K-15K sweet spot per person
- Decision cycle: 2-7 days from first inquiry
- Trust signals: real trip photos, friend referrals, WhatsApp conversation > website reviews

## Seasonality
- **Peak:** Oct-Feb (winter trips — Rajasthan, Kashmir), Apr-Jun (summer trips — Himachal, Uttarakhand)
- **Off-peak:** Jul-Sep (monsoon — except Ladakh/Spiti which peak Jun-Sep)
- **Booking lead time:** 7-21 days before trip date

## Regulatory
- IRCTC licensing for domestic packages
- GST implications on tour packages (5% composite vs 18% regular)
- Travel insurance not mandatory but recommended for treks
- Inner-line permits required for Spiti, Ladakh, parts of Arunachal

## Tech Trends (2025-2026)
- WhatsApp Business API adoption for booking (replacing call centers)
- AI chatbots handling 60-80% of pre-sales inquiries
- UPI/payment gateway integration (Razorpay, PhonePe, GPay)
- Google Maps API for trip visualization and itinerary mapping
- Instagram Reels as primary discovery channel for Gen-Z travelers

## Content Strategy Patterns
- User-generated content (trip photos/reels from past travelers)
- Destination guides → SEO traffic → WhatsApp lead capture
- WhatsApp Status marketing (stories from ongoing trips)
- Instagram Reels showing trip highlights (15-30 sec)
- YouTube vlogs from trip captains/travelers
