### Global FAQ Section (Use on all pages)
**Headline:** Frequently Asked Questions
**Sub-headline:** about Corporate Tax & Accounting:
**Q1:** How are bookkeeping and accounting different?
**A1:** Bookkeeping focuses on recording daily financial transactions and managing invoices, while accounting involves interpreting, classifying, and analyzing that financial data to provide strategic business insights.
**Q2:** What is a Virtual CFO?
**A2:** A Virtual CFO provides high-level financial strategy, cash flow management, and financial forecasting for growing businesses on a part-time or project basis, saving the cost of a full-time executive.
**Q3:** When is a Statutory Audit mandatory?
**A3:** Under the Companies Act, every Private and Public Limited Company in India must undergo a statutory audit annually, regardless of their turnover, capital, or profit/loss status.
**Q4:** How often should we file GST returns?
**A4:** Most businesses are required to file GST returns monthly (GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B). However, small businesses under the QRMP scheme may file quarterly, with an annual reconciliation return.
**Q5:** Can you represent us in tax disputes?
**A5:** Yes, our specialized litigation team represents clients before tax authorities, handling scrutiny notices, CIT(A) appeals, and ITAT representations for both direct and indirect tax matters.
